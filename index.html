Education

High School

I attended Dublin High School and Menlo-Atherton High School in California. I transferred from Dublin High to Menlo-Atherton High in the middle of my third year and graduated in 2017.

University

I was admitted in Fall 2017 and graduated in May 2021 from San Francisco State University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and a Minor in Mathematics.

SF Muni Internship

While in University, I was an Intern for the San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency (SFMTA) working on various projects for the City of San Francisco and its transit system.

Waymo

After graduating from University, I was offered a contract position through Collabera, Inc. as a Software Quality Operations Associate to support the self-driving robotaxi company, Waymo. My job was to triage and provide feedback with the collected data from the Waymo cars to improve safety and better driving behavior.

Nuro

I currently work as Command Center Operator for Nuro, a self-driving software company looking to better everyday life with robotics. My job is to overlook and teach our robots better driving behavior through test requests and data collection.

Services

UI/UX Design

Throughout my time in University, I grew an interest in UI/UX Design. I took a class in Web Development and Human-Computer Interaction. The Web Development class introduced me to HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP, where we designed our own websites/portfolios. In my Human-Computer Interaction class, I learned how to design websites that are user friendly and what aspects of a website are more appealing to the human eye.

Machine Learning

My interest in Machine Learning and Artifical Intelligence has grown ever since I joined the Autonomous Driving Vehicle industry with Waymo and Nuro. I have learned to develop projects that allow the computer to think on its own, such as pedicting stock prices and multiple calculator variations. The main language that I use for Machine Learning is Python.

Data Analysis

Quality Testing
